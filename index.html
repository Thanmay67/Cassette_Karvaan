Who We Are

The Story of
karvaan

Cassette karvaan is a fresh, passionate band born out of a shared love for the timeless sounds. We believe music is a journey — a karvaan — that carries stories, nostalgia, and pure joy.

We started small, with a few friends who couldn't stop humming old film melodies. That hum turned into rehearsals, rehearsals turned into performances, and now we're bringing these golden songs to live audiences in Mysuru and beyond.

📼

Our Roots

Inspired by the golden eras of Hindi and Kannada film music — from 70s classics to modern Sandalwood chartbusters. Every note we play carries the soul of these beloved soundtracks.
