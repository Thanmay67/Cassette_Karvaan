Who We Are

The Story of
Karavaan

Cassette Karavaan is a fresh, passionate cover band born out of a shared love for the timeless sounds of Bollywood and Sandalwood cinema. We believe music is a journey — a karavaan — that carries stories, nostalgia, and pure joy.

We started small, with a few friends who couldn't stop humming old film melodies. That hum turned into rehearsals, rehearsals turned into performances, and now we're bringing these golden songs to live audiences in Mysuru and beyond.

Though we're just getting started, our café shows have already been filled with warmth, singalongs, and the magic that only live music can bring.
